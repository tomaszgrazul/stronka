Biography

I live in Wroclaw, Poland and I am a self-taught artist.

My first oil painting I created when I was 13 years old.

At the beginning I was learning to paint by copying famous artists works.

In the following years I practised pencil sketching, loving the precision of detail work. I fell in love in painting even more.

Along with my far distance travels and interests in yoga, eastern philosophy and meditation, the huge passion for acrylic mandalas painting arose in my heart .

Inspired by the work of the Canadian artist Sarah McKendry I finished one of her online courses in naturalistic oil painting technique. I intend to continue learning on her next classes to achieve much more better skills

Sitting and painting in front of the easel I am moving to another world...

where time doesn't exist and my soul is blooming and wanting to create even more.
